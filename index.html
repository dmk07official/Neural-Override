What is Neural Override

Neural Override was started by our Frontman, who goes by the same name – How creative.

At first, he was all into the E-Punk and Incelcore scene, dropping tracks on YouTube and later SoundCloud.

About three months later, he hit me up, dmk07, to join Neural Override. We already did a feature together before.

Once I joined, we gone away from E-punk and more to the indie and alternative rock genres.

Right now, we're deep in the process of discovering our own unique style. And it will hit like a cold beer after a long day

Our first tracks will be dropping on Spotify soon. Until then, get an early taste of the vibe on SoundCloud, where our frontman Neural Override has been experimenting over the past few months.

For more informations about us both, check out the members page and for the latest news the news page.

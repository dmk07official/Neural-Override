Our Mission

We originally aimed to be part of the E-Punk scene – but somewhere along the way, we realized it wasn’t truly *us*. Right now, we're deep in the process of discovering our own unique style. Now, our mission is to share this sound with the world.
The first tracks of this type are coming soon on Spotify. Until then, get an early taste of the vibe on SoundCloud, where our frontman Neural Override has been experimenting over the past few months.

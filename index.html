About Neural Override

The Beginning

Neural Override started making music under this name back in February 2025.

At first, he was all into the E-Punk and Alternative scene, dropping tracks on YouTube and later on SoundCloud.

About three months later, he hit me up (Dominik) to work on a feature (Alcoholic Therapy), and from there on, I became a part of his music journey.

We were thinking about making an album and how to get our music to you. During that period of time, I created this website, and Neural Override started to connect to the Incelcore scene and worked on some features and compilations with some cool people.

Now

In November 2025, Neural Override released his first single (I Know That I Want You) on Spotify. He started to make serious progress and improvements and is currently working on his first EP, Unloveable.

The genre of his tracks has shifted more to Incelcore on tracks he works on alone, while when we are working on a song together, it's more of a Bedroom Rock Pop vibe.

For more informations about us both, check out the members page and for the latest news about the band check our news page.
